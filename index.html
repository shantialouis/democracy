APPRAISAL OF THE YOUTHS IN THE CONSOLIDATION OF DEMOCRACY.

This chapter examines the role of youth in the consolidation of Democracy in Nigeria.

The participation of the youths in the democratization process contributes to the development of democracy in Nigeria. This Article traces the background of the youth participation to the earlier times when they formed associations which later transformed into political parties. Right from the period of colonialism, during the struggles against it, Nigerian youth were in the forefront. The formation of West African Students Union (WASU) and the Nigerian Youth Movement (NYM), is a testimony to the significant role played by the youth in the struggle against colonialism.
